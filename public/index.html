PROJECT

RMDb is an online database of reviews for movies. Any user is allowed to browse through and read reviews for latest movies by fellow users and also critics. Users can also check movies' IMDB ratings and give their own rating. Users can find and follow friends on the website. When a user feels confident about reviewing, they can apply to be a critic. Admins can approve or reject a movie critic written by a user. Approved user can write reviews for movies which will be prioritized to other users.
